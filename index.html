What Do You Want

Why should I use WdyW?

We know that life can be hard, and sometimes, it becomes hard to keep up. We want to make life simpler for you. We want you to have money as and when you want it. We believe that money should not stop a person from getting what s/he desires or needs. And we hate stress. With WdyW, users can repay a borrowed amount as per their convenience. Our simple, one-touch features are designed to be easy to understand. After all, why waste time reading the small text?.

How is it different from my mobile wallet?

Mobile wallet is only one feature of ours. We offer a lot more services than just sharing money, like NFC payment, group payments, automatic bill-payment and lending-borrowing. Most importantly, we have introduced the concept of banking to the online world. Download the WdyW© app today to carry your bank in your pocket :D

What is the NFC payment?

Near field Communication. Coming soon. We are still working to perfect this for you.

How can I return my loan?

Easy repayment method. Single/multiple installments as you wish. Pay whenever you want to. Ample time will be given to repay your loan.

How do I know if I can trust a money-borrower?

WdyW will provide every user with a credit score. Good score means reliable user. Bad score is a defaulter. Obviously. Plus there's much more. Don't Worry.
